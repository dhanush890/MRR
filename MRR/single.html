Manpower planning, Recruitment, Selection and Placement

1. Reviewing the manpower availability and requirements.
2. Note sending to Management for approval.
3. Posting, Screening, Short Listing, Telephonic and Scheduling Interview & Selection.
4. Release of Advertisement in leading Newspapers.
5. Co-coordinating with the advertisement agency regarding the release of the material.
6. Searching relevant experience candidates through Websites & Portals.
7. Co-ordinating with agencies for Recruitment.
8. Collecting the relevant experience candidates resume.
9. Preparing list of sorted candidates for the Interview.
10. Preparing Schedule of the Interview.
11. Sending Call letters to the selected candidates.
12. Preparation of list of Selected Candidates.
13. Taking care of Document verification, Offer letter preparation.
14. Taking care of Joining formalities, Induction, Bank Account opening.
15. Monitoring Medical Fitness with Doctor Certificate.
16. Issuing Preliminary Appointment Order to the selected candidates.
17. Preparing Appointment orders.
18. Explaining Rules, Regulations & Procedures to all new employees.
19. Taking care of Personal File maintenance.

Training and Development

1. Sending Training Need Assessment form to the concerned HODs.
2. Monitoring coordinating with all departments.
3. Preparation of consolidated statement Programme wise/Department wise.
4. Identification of Training needs.
5. Arranging for training from outside trainers (Resource Person).
6. Nomination of Employees to the training programme after getting the approval from the Management.
7. Preparing Training Calendar for the year.
8. Preparing Training Budget for the year.
9. Monitoring & Maintenance of Training Budget.

Probation and Confirmation

1. Preparing Probations List.
2. Maintenance of Probation Register.
3. Preparing list of Rewards & recognition of employees department wise.
4. Confirmation Report sending to the Concerned HODs.
5. Monitoring to collecting the same from the HODs.
6. Preparing Confirmation Orders based upon the HODs Reports.
7. Issue of Confirmation Order to the Employees through Concerned HODs.

Performance Management System(PMS)

1. Sending the Performance Appraisals of the employees to the concerned HODs.
2. Monitoring to collecting the same from the HODs.
3. Preparing Performance Appraisal Report Level wise/Department wise.
4. Preparing the list of the promotion quarterly.

Extension / Termination - Contract

1. Co-ordinating with the HODs and collect the Report for Termination / Extension of contract.
2. Sending note to the management for the Extension / Termination of Contract for contract Employees.
3. Preparing Extension / Termination Order.
4. Issue of Extension / Termination Order to the Contract Employees.

Resignation and Final Settlements

1. Co-ordinating with Exit Interviews.
2. Sending a copy of the Resignation Letter to the Management for Approval.
3. Information to Acceptance of Resignation Letter to the Resigned Employee.
4. Collecting Clearance Certificate for the concerned resigned employees through concerned Department Heads.
5. Informing the resigned employee about his dues and collecting the same.
6. Preparing Relieving order and Service Certificate.
7. Issuing Reliving Order and Service Certificate to the resigned employees.

Employee relations and Welfare

1. Investigate complaints and coordinate with other Departments.
2. Taking Care of Employees’ Working Environment.
3. Giving Building Access for new ID Cards.
4. Taking Care of Employees’ Attendance System/Management.
5. Maintenance of Leave Management System.
6. Taking Necessary action for Late Comers.
7. Monitoring Absenteeism and taking necessary action to minimize.
8. Monitoring Canteen Facilities and Cleanliness with Admin.
9. Coordinating and Organizing Cross Sectional Meeting to giving an opportunity to all employees to raise their queries with the Management
10. Responsible for handling the functions of Statutory Compliance and Labour laws for the rmployees.

Compensation and Benefits / Payroll processing

1. Creating the Employees’ salary history into the database.
2. Incentive calculation monthly, quarter and half yearly pertaining to particular financial year.
3. Collecting and updating various Department inputs.
4. Monthly Salary preparation and Payslip preparation.
5. Updating employees Income Tax investments into the database.
6. Processing salary and cross checking with the variance analysis.
7. Preparing checklist of the employees’ salary for reconciliation cross check 8. Co-ordinating with Finance Department for the Employees’ Salary Cheque.
9. Taking care of Employees’ salary credit in to their respective salary account.
10. Preparing Bonus Statement once in a Year.
11. Preparing Increment letter and increment calculations.
12. Calculations of leave encashment and Full and final settlements.

Statutory Compliances

1. Preparing Annual and Half Yearly Returns of Factory Inspectorate.
2. Preparing renewal of Factory Licence.
3. Preparing From V list of National & Festival holidays.
4. PF & ESI Nomination for new joinees.
5. Preparing Annual & Monthly Returns of PF.
6. Preparing Half Yearly & Monthly Retuns of ESI.
7. Preparing annual returns of Bonus.
8. Preparing Monthly and annual returns of Professional Tax.
9. Taking Care of PF Settlement for resigned Employees.
10. Taking Care of PF Transfer for New Joiners.
11. Taking Care of ESI Compliance and Queries.

Professional Development / Human Resource Management

1. One to One session with all staff to identify problems if any and take corrective action for the same.
2. Taking care of Manpower Statistics.
3. Implementation of policies and procedure.
4. Introducing New Human Resources Management Software for making Paperless office.
5. Monitor the effectiveness of HR policies and recommend changes as appropriate provide information, statistics and reports management.
